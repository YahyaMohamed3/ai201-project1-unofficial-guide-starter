OVERVIEW

The AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate is focused on the design of
cost and performance optimized solutions. It is an ideal starting point for
candidates with AWS Cloud or strong on-premises IT experience. This exam does
not require deep hands-on coding experience, although familiarity with basic
programming concepts is an advantage.

This certification is ideal for:

* Cloud professionals looking to validate their ability to design Well-Architected solutions
* Candidates with 1-3 years of IT experience starting their AWS Cloud journey
* Individuals seeking increased credibility with technical IT/cloud colleagues
and customers

EXAM DETAILS

* Category: Associate
* Duration: 130 minutes
* Format: 65 questions, multiple choice or multiple response
* Cost: $150 USD
* Testing: Pearson VUE testing center or online proctored exam
* Validity: 3 years

WHO SHOULD TAKE IT

Per the exam guide, the recommended experience prior to taking this exam is at
least 1 year of hands-on experience designing cloud solutions that use AWS services.
Candidates who do not have any IT work experience would benefit from first earning
the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner before attempting this certification.

HOW TO PREPARE (4-STEP PLAN)

Step 1 - Know the exam: Review the official exam guide. Take the AWS
Certification Official Practice Question Set to understand exam-style questions.

Step 2 - Build knowledge: Enroll in digital courses where you need to fill gaps
in knowledge and skills. Practice hands-on with AWS Builder Labs, AWS Cloud Quest,
and AWS Jam.

Step 3 - Review and practice: Explore each exam domain's topics and how they
align to AWS services. Reinforce your knowledge and identify learning gaps with
exam-style questions and flashcards. Continue practicing with AWS Builder Labs,
AWS SimuLearn, and/or AWS Escape Room.

Step 4 - Assess readiness: Take the AWS Certification Official Practice Exam
before scheduling the real exam.

NEXT STEPS AFTER EARNING THIS CERT

Common next certifications:

* AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Professional (to advance further in
solutions architect roles)
* AWS Certified Security - Specialty (to build advanced technical skills in
securing workloads and architectures)
* AWS Certified Data Engineer - Associate (to advance into roles like cloud
data engineer)
