OVERVIEW

Tech Interview Handbook is a free, curated resource for technical interview
preparation. Created by Yangshun Tay, ex-Meta Staff Engineer and author of
Blind 75. Over 1,000,000 people have used this resource.

The handbook covers all phases of a technical interview: applying for a job,
passing interviews, and offer negotiation.

WHAT IS COVERED

- Coding interview best practices: do's and don'ts before, during, and after
- Grind 75: curated LeetCode questions prioritized by importance
- Algorithm cheatsheets categorized by topic
- Step-by-step software engineer resume guide
- Behavioral questions asked by top tech companies
- Coding interview study plan with recommended weekly schedule

WHO IT IS FOR

- Candidates new to technical interviews
- Experienced engineers returning to the job market
- Anyone who wants to improve their technical interviewing skills

KEY PHILOSOPHY

Focus on practical content over just links. Covers algorithm questions plus
non-technical and domain-specific interview content. Consistent practice is
the key to success, not memorizing answers.

TOOLS MENTIONED

- Grind 75: study plan generator based on time available
- Blind 75: original curated list of top LeetCode questions
- AlgoMonster: pattern-based prep by Google engineers
- interviewing.io: mock interviews with real engineers anonymously
