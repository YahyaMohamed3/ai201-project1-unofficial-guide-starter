OVERVIEW

The AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner validates foundational, high-level
understanding of AWS Cloud, services, and terminology. It is a good starting
point for individuals with no prior IT or cloud experience, or for
non-technical employees who want cloud literacy.

This certification is ideal for:
- People switching careers into cloud from non-IT backgrounds
- Line-of-business employees (sales, marketing, project management) who want
  to communicate more effectively with technical teams
- Anyone wanting an entry-level credential before pursuing associate-level certs

EXAM DETAILS

- Category: Foundational
- Duration: 90 minutes
- Format: 65 questions, multiple choice or multiple response
- Cost: $100 USD
- Testing: Pearson VUE testing center or online proctored exam
- Validity: 3 years

WHO SHOULD TAKE IT

Designed for candidates new to cloud who may not have an IT background.
Up to 6 months of AWS exposure is helpful but not required.

HOW TO PREPARE (4-STEP PLAN)

Step 1 - Know the exam: Review the official exam guide. Take the AWS
Certification Official Practice Question Set and Pretest to identify gaps.

Step 2 - Build knowledge: Enroll in digital courses. Practice hands-on with
AWS Builder Labs, AWS Cloud Quest, and AWS Jam.

Step 3 - Review and practice: Study each exam domain. Use exam-style questions
and flashcards. Practice with AWS Builder Labs and AWS SimuLearn.

Step 4 - Assess readiness: Take the AWS Certification Official Practice Exam
before scheduling the real exam.

NEXT STEPS AFTER EARNING THIS CERT

Common next certifications:
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (cloud architect roles)
- AWS Certified Developer - Associate (developer roles)
- AWS Certified SysOps Administrator - Associate (systems administrator roles)

These associate-level certifications advance you into cloud architect, cloud
engineer, developer, and systems administrator careers.
